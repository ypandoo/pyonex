失眠

Insomnia is a general term for issues with one’s sleep. Its symptoms include a difficulty to fall asleep, lying awake for a long while at night, waking up several times at night, not being able to get back to sleep after waking up early in the morning and the like. It affects not only one’s sleep but also his/her next day.
