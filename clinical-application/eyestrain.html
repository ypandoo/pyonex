EYESTRAIN

Eyestrain is not an eye disease but a group of symptoms occurring as a result of an extended use of eyes. A common experience may be extended computer use, which symptoms include headaches, blurry vision, and other discomfort such as feeling of dryness.

(supreme Yang)
