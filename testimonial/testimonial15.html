Advantages of 新的Pyonex

  1. Painfree application of the 新的Pyonex 0.3 and 0.6, therefore particularly good for the treatment of babies and children. 无痛技术的0.3和0.6的新的Pyonex对婴儿和儿童的治疗特别好。
  2. Adhesive does not come off during showering or bathing. 洗澡或淋浴时胶带不会脱落

How I use 新的Pyonex, Clinical Application of 新的Pyonex 关于我使用新的Pyonex针的临床应用

I treat 8 to 15 children in my clinic every day. The use of press tacks in addition to Japanese pediatric Shonishin and laser acupuncture has proven to be very helpful. The 新的Pyonex needles with a length of 0.3 and 0.6 mm are particularly useful. 每天有8-15个小孩需要在我这边看诊，按压针的使用已被证实是非常有效除了日本小孩Shonishin和激光针灸疗法。尤其是0.3厘米和0。6里面的新的Pyonex针非常有效。

Since the skin of children, and that of babies in particular, is considerably thinner than that of adults and therefore much more sensitive, the shortest needle with a length of not more than 0.3 mm is suited particularly well for the treatment of babies. At this length the needle reaches only the upper epidermal layer (thickness of the epidermis in the first month after birth is between 50 and 55 μm) and does not penetrate. This layer is free of cutaneous nerve plexi and blood vessels so the application of these needles is non-invasive and gentle, especially since it does not produce any discomfort or pain. For this reason the 0.3 mm 新的Pyonex particularly suited for the treatment of newborn babies and infants. 由于儿童的皮肤，特别是婴儿的皮肤，比成人的要薄得多，因此也要敏感得多，长度不超过0.3毫米的短针特别适用于对婴儿的治疗。针的长度仅触及都上表皮层 （出生后第一个月的表皮厚度在50至55μm之间），且不穿透。这一层是无皮神经丛和血管，所以这些针的应用是非侵入性式的和非常温和，特别是因为它不产生任何不适或疼痛。 为此，0.3毫米新的针术尤其适用于新生儿和婴儿治疗。

The main application of 新的Pyonex for newborns and babies is digestive problems. During the first months after birth the so-called “three-month colic” is mainly responsible for screaming fits. In this case treatment with a press tack is very helpful. During the Shonishin training I offer midwives, I teach them the application of the 0.3 mm 新的Pyonex for digestive problems – in particular for infantile colic. This is part of the standard training and the midwives who clinically apply 新的Pyonex do appreciate it. 新的Pyonex针对新生儿和婴儿的主要应用是消化问题。出生后的头几个月，所谓的“三个月绞痛”新生儿的主要反应是通过大声尖叫。在这种情况下，用压舌钉治疗非常有帮助。 我对shonishin培训提供助产期间，我教他们应用0.3毫米新的Pyonex作用于治疗消化系统的问题尤其是小儿疝气。这是对于那些感谢于Pyonex并临床运用Pyonex针的接生员的训练标准的一部分。

The needles can be applied without any problems on both sides of the navel in the region of the acupoint ST-25. Here the 新的Pyonex shows its advantage. Because their non-invasive properties, not even the diaper waistband putting pressure on the skin and the moist heat developing under it lead to any degree of irritation in the area of the press tack or the plaster. Another advantage of the 新的Pyonex is that bathing and washing doesn’t cause the press tack to detach. Apart from infantile colics, the 0.3 mm 新的Pyonex needles are useful in the treatment of babies with marked vomiting as well as reflux. In both cases the needles are applied to Ren-12. For insomnia the points Ren-15 and BL-15 are treated with the 0.3 mm 新的Pyonex. 针能够在肚脐两边ST-25穴位区域进行针灸不会有任何问题。这就恰好体现了新的Pyonex的优势。因为他们是无创伤的针头，甚至连尿布腰带对皮肤施加压力 和在其下形成的湿热度都不会导致在按压针或膏药的部位产生任何程度的刺激。该新的Pyonex针的另一个优势是，沐浴和洗澡都不会使按压针脱落。 0.3毫米新的Pyonex针在治疗反胃和呕吐的婴儿是非常有效的除了婴儿腹痛。在这两种情况下的针应用于ren-12。失眠的分ren-15和bl-15与0.3毫米新的针灸治疗。

The advantage of the 新的Pyonex over other commercially available press tacks becomes obvious especially in the treatment of babies. Apart from pain-free application, 新的Pyonex allows parents to be included in the treatment of their child. Parents may administer a gentle stimulation through the needle, when they apply a light circling pressure over the press tacks at home. This is done most conveniently during the regular diaper changes. With other press tack needles this would quickly lead to overstimulation - a danger not associated with the 0.3 mm 新的Pyonex. 该新的Pyonex在其他市场上可以买得到的按压针的优势变得明显，尤其是在治疗的宝宝。除了无痛技术，新的Pyonex在治疗孩子的同时也治疗了父母， 父母可能会使用温和的针灸方式，当他们把光环压在压钉在家里。在定期换尿布时这是最方便的。与其他按压针会迅速导致过度刺激-不与0.3毫米新的Pyonex相关。

As a general rule the time press tacks are left in place for pediatric treatment is as follows: They should be removed after three days, and this can be done without any problems by the parents. For older children, with diseases of the musculoskeketal system, a more intense stimulation of acupoints, Ahshi points, and relevant trigger points is required. In cases of cerebral palsy (spastic form) for example, the absolutely pain-free application of the 0.6 mm 新的Pyonex at the acupoints SP-9 has been very helpful in achieving a reduction of the adductor muscle spasms. 通常情况下，儿科治疗时，按压针是留在原处的情况如下：他们应该在三天后被摘除，在没有家长的情况下这样做没有任何问题。对于年龄较大的儿童， 在musculoskeketal系统疾病，更为强烈的刺激穴位，Ahshi穴，和相关的触发穴是必需的。在脑瘫病例（痉挛型）为例， 对0.6毫米新的Pyonex针穴位sp-9的无痛技术在降低内收肌痉挛时很有帮助。

In cases of general increase in muscle tension, LR-3 is treated to relieve the increase in tension. Contracture in arms (flexion deformity) can be treated by placing 0.6 or 0.9 mm 新的Pyonex at LI-11, and likewise for contracture in the legs can be treated with GB-34. Through the prolonged application of press tacks, the dispersion effect on the acupoints can be intensified without causing overstimulation. These press tacks should be removed after 3-5 days. If continued treatment with the press tacks is indicated, they can be reapplied after a 3-day interval. 在肌肉肌肉增加的通常情况下，LR-3穴作用于对肌肉紧张的缓解。挛缩的手臂（屈曲畸形）可贴上0.6或0.9毫米的新的Pyonex在li-11穴位，同样的，gb-34穴可治疗腿肌挛缩。 通过按压针的长期作用，对穴位的色散效应可以强化不造成过度刺激。这些按压针应该在3-5天内摘除。如果按压针一直不摘除则表明，他们可以被重置 在3天的间隔之后。

The press tack needles are also very useful in the treatment of adults. It is advantageous particularly when timely acupuncture treatment is not possible due to scheduling constraints – when a patient has to be fitted somewhere into our busy schedule due to the sudden onset of pain, for example. In this case the treatment of ashi points with the press tack needles alone can take the edge off the pain. 按压针在成人的治疗中也非常有效。这是有很有利的，特别是当由于没空而不能及时进行针灸治疗的时候，例如，由于突然发作的疼痛，病人需要我们，但我们恰好很忙的时候。 在这种情况下按压针单独治疗ashi穴就可以减轻疼痛。

The following has been helpful in general regarding press tack length in the treatment adults: Patients with complaints in the lumbar region react more sensitively to a needle prick in the lumbar region than in the shoulder region. This is due to the density of pain receptors, which is higher in the lumbar region than in the neck and shoulder region. For this reason, adults with acute lumbar pain who are pain sensitive for example, are treated with the 0.6 mm New Pyonex, while the 0.9 mm 新的Pyonex is used for pain in the neck and shoulder region. Patients who are less pain sensitive may be treated with the 0.9 mm 新的Pyonex (lumbar region) and the 1.2 mm 新的Pyonex (shoulder-neck region). 一般情况下，在治疗成年人时，关于按压针长度在以下说明中是非常有帮助的：腰椎患者对腰部针刺伤的反应比肩部更敏感。这是和疼痛受体的密度有关， 腰部比颈部和肩部区域更为敏感。因此，急性腰部疼痛更为敏感的成年人应用0.6毫米的新Pyonex针进行针灸治疗，而0.9毫米新的Pyonex针用于颈部和肩部疼痛的治疗。 没那么疼痛敏感的患者可采用0.9 mm新的针术治疗（腰部）和1.2毫米新的针术（肩颈部）。
