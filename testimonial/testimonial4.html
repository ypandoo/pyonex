Callison, Matt

M.S. L.Ac
President of Sports Medicine Acupuncture Certification Program

Acupuncture During Exercise

Amongst all of the different techniques of acupuncture, using 新的Pyonex press needles by Seirin affords the practitioner a unique and effective tool when it comes to combining acupuncture treatment with rehabilitative exercises. Seirin uses a high quality stainless steel needle with a hypo-allergenic porous adhesive tape that keeps the needle in proper position even in the most active patients. I have used many different press needles in my 20 years of combining acupuncture and exercise and I have found that 新的Pyonex needles are the best quality and pain-free. The 新的Pyonex press needles come in .20 gauge and range in length from 0.3 mm to 1.5 mm. For acupuncture stimulation during exercise I prefer to use either the 0.9mm or 1.2mm press needles. I have used the 0.6 mm needles on sensitive patients and they consistently report no discomfort during any of their rehabilitative exercises. Sending patients home with 新的Pyonex press needle so that they can perform their home exercises while receiving a “treatment” can reduce rehabilitation times. 在所有的针灸的不同技术之间，通过使用Seirin提供的医生新的Pyonex按压针，有效结合了针灸疗法和康复训练的独特工具。Seirin采用高质量的 不锈钢针和低过敏的多孔胶带以便于将针保持在最合适的位置，及时在活跃的病人身上依旧能稳固一个恰当的位置。在我的针灸和复健的20年生涯里，我使用 过大大小小多种不同的按压针，我发现新的Pyonex才是无痛技术做得最好且质量也很高的一款产品。新的Pyonex按压针达到20G，并且长度范围在0.3毫米至1.5毫米之间。 对于在我训练时使用的针灸刺激来说，我更偏好喜欢使用0.9毫米或1.2毫米的按压针。我已经在敏感的病人身上使用过0.6毫米的针，他们都一直表示在康复训练时没有引起任何的不适。 使用新的Pyonex按压针就可以让病人在家里操作了。让病人能够回家练习就可以减少病人接受“治疗”的康复时间。

Strategies for 新的Pyonex Press Needle Use 新Pyonex按压针的使用策略

The practitioner may choose to use these press needles on points that were already stimulated in the main treatment in order to reinforce the effects of that treatment, or they may choose an entirely new set of points with a slightly different effect or focus. The following are different examples of using 新的Pyonex press needles as an adjunctive treatment for musculoskeletal injury rehabilitation and during athletic events. 在主要的治疗里，医生可能只会选择使用这些按压针在已经刺激过的穴位上，仅便于加强治疗效果，或者医生也可以选择一个全新的不同作用点的穴位。 以下是使用新的Pyonex按压针对于肌肉骨骼损伤的康复辅助治疗在体育项目中的不同例子。

  • The practitioner may choose to use 新的Pyonex needles as an adjunctive treatment that focuses on reducing pain and easing tension in the channels and collaterals. For example, a patient with lateral epicondylitis will often have less pain after placing a press needle at LI 2 (erjian) or LI 3 (sanjian). 医生可能会选择使用新的Pyonex针作为一种辅助治疗手段，以专注于减轻疼痛和舒缓经络紧张。例如一个有肱骨外上髁炎的患者，将按压针放在二建或三建上， 往往是会感觉不到疼痛的
  • With musculoskeletal injury rehabilitation such as with an acute injury, the practitioner can apply 新的Pyonex press needles on the well-jing and cleft-xi points of the affected channel(s) to help successfully rid the channel(s) of obstructions. 例如像有一个急性损伤的肌肉骨骼损伤的康复治疗患者，医生可以将Pyonex按压针施加在井穴或受影响经络管道的cleft-xi穴，能成功帮助摆脱经络管道的堵塞。
  • During rehabilitative exercises, the practitioner can apply 新的Pyonex needles to extraordinary vessel (qi jing ba mai) points and motor points to constantly stimulate proprioception and enhance the flow of qi and blood in the channels. 在康复训练中，医生可以将新的Pyonex按压针作用在一个特殊血管的（七经八脉）穴位和运动的穴位上，以便不断激发自我感受和加强气血在经络管道中的流通。
  • During athletic events there are a few point prescriptions that are successful at subjectively increasing endurance and agility. One such prescription is the application of 新的Pyonex press needles to specific points: Auricular: Lung point, DU 14 (dazhui), ST 36 (zusanli) and UB 58 (feiyang). 在运动项目中，的确有几点穴位处方可以主观层面上成功地提高耐力和敏捷力。这样的处方也的确是新的Pyonex按压针在特殊穴位的一个应用。 Auricular: Lung point, DU 14 (dazhui), ST 36 (zusanli) and UB 58 (feiyang)

Used as a primary treatment or post-treatment, 新的Pyonex needles are the best press needles when it comes to patient comfort and continued stimulation of the energetic systems. 当按压针能够带给患者一种舒服和持续对能量系统进行刺激时，新的Pyonex针可以说是无论在治疗前还是治疗后都是最好的按压针。
