Römer, Ansgar

Dr. med.,Gynecologist and Obstetrician医学博士，妇产科医师。
Medical training officer at the medical training society Pro Medico

Advantages of 新的Pyonex 新的Pyonex的优势

This new press tack can be ideally combined with body and auricular acupuncture. This enables simple, reliable and gentle treatment with specific advantages in addition to the exact and particularly long-lasting adhesive properties, which have been further improved over the former product.
The advantages of the 新的Pyonex press tacks for the daily clinical work of therapists regarding practicality, safety, efficiency and patient friendliness are obvious and will be readily assessable to every new user. 这种新款按压针可以完美地将耳穴和身体结合起来。除了在进一步改善旧产品后出现的持久粘性特性的新产品之外，这还使得简单可靠温和的治疗 有着特别的优势所在。
新的Pyonex按压针的优势在于它适用于医生日常临床实践工作。

How I use the 新的Pyonex, Clinical Application of 新的Pyonex 我该如何使用新的Pyonex，和新的Pyonex的临床应用

The effects of the 新的Pyonex press tacks has been proven especially in the field of gynecology and obstetrics. Based on the high effectiveness the combined use of press tacks and body acupuncture should be considered, in particular for chronic complaints. 新的Pyonex按压针的效果尤其是在妇科和妇产科领域已经得到认可。现在应该考虑基于按压针和身体穴位的高效结合利用，尤其是一些慢性疾病。

Patients benefit from the application of 新的Pyonex since they are safe, hygienic and very simple to use. The plaster is very skin-friendly and breathable so therefore, based on my clinical experience, it rarely causes any skin reactions even in patients with extremely sensitive skin. These press tacks allow an precise, long-lasting acupoint placement.
The duration of the application can usually be directed as being up to one week. 患者使用新的Pyonex的好处就是它安全，卫生，易于操作。石膏材料非常适合接触皮肤，并且有很好的透气性。因此，基于我的临床实践，它几乎不会造成 任何皮肤不良反应，甚至在极度敏感的患者皮肤身上。这些按压针可以长期精确地贴在穴位上。
在使用期间可以经常起到长达一周的效果。

This is indicated for conditions like pregnancy induced carpal tunnel syndrome, hyperemesis gravidarum and back pain, but it is also effective for headache and pubic symphysis pain. It is ideal and most effective when this application is combined with or alternated with body acupuncture therapy. This safe, painless and pleasant-to-the-patient application demonstrably increases the effectiveness of body acupuncture in the interval between treatments. 这就表明了像催产，腕管综合征，妊娠呕吐和背痛等情况下能起到效果，也对头痛，耻骨联合疼痛有效果。当这种方式结合了身体穴位的疗法将变得更理想和高效。 这种安全无痛患者也很满意的产品明显提高了身体穴位的治疗效果。

The 新的Pyonex can be used at PC-6 for carpal tunnel syndrome, hyperemesis gravidarum. The 新的Pyonex has also been proven useful at the points REN-12 and ST-36 for the treatment of nausea of pregnancy. 新的Pyonex可以作用于PC-6穴位来治疗腕管综合征，妊娠呕吐。新的Pyonex也被证实了在REN-12和ST-36穴位治疗妊娠恶心干呕是非常见效的。

The frequent complaint of back pain during pregnancy is an ideal and most effective condition for the application of 新的Pyonex. Acupoints in the region of BL-25 to 34 should be considered as well as DU-3 or 4 and possible distant points like BL-40 and 60 and GB-34. The efficacy and duration of effect of regular acupuncture for this condition is considered to be great, but the effect can be further increased and the time it takes for the effects to manifest can be shortened with the use of 新的Pyonex. 新的Pyonex在针对于妊娠期间的频发性背脊痛是非常有效果的。应该考虑到针灸在BL-25至34区域或者DU-3或4区域或者是更远一点的BL-40和60穴或GB-34穴。 这样下来，常规普通的针灸虽然也会有很好的疗效。但使用新的Pyonex可以将效果有进一步的提升并且疗程也会缩短。

In cases of headache, pharmacotherapy is often not an option during pregnancy. The use of acupuncture is therefore both necessary and effective. Treatment with the addition of individually selected distant points like GB-41, LI-3, ST-36, ST-44, LI-4 and PC-6 can be ideally combined with the application of 新的Pyonex. The augmentation of therapeutic effects is demonstrated here also in my daily clinical practice. Pain in the pubic symphysis region can be a great burden for pregnant women. The application of the 新的Pyonex at the acupoints Ren-2, KI-11, ST-30 or local Ahshi points is recommended depending on the degree of the pain complaint. The combination with body acupuncture increases the effect in cases of extreme pain. 在怀孕期间，头痛或心理疾病往往不会出现，因此就体现了针灸作用的必要性。分别挑选像GB-14,LI-3,ST-36,ST-44,LI-4和PC-6这样的一些疏远的穴位 进行加剂治疗可以完美地和新的Pyonex进行结合。强化疗效被证实。耻骨联合区域的疼痛感在孕妇身上可能会被放大。根据疼痛的剧烈程度，推荐将 新的Pyonex针灸贴在Ren-2，KI-11,ST-30穴或局部的Ahshi穴结合身体穴位。如果是剧烈疼痛的话，与针灸结合可以增强疗效。

The role of physicians and midwifes during labor is to maintain the mobility of the women, and positively support delivery. Body acupuncture can be a hindrance under certain circumstances in this regard. Instead, for cases of mild discomfort and labor pains, which is felt particularly in the lumbar and sacral areas, the application of the 新的Pyonex in the region between BL-23 and BL34, Du-3 and Du-4 as well as local Ahshi points is recommended. 医生或接产的护士在接产期间会保持女性流动性，并且能够明确地支持生产。在某种确定的环境下，体针可能会被视为一种累赘。然而，对于一些轻度不适 或者分娩时的疼痛尤其是腰骶部位感觉到的疼痛，这里推荐使用新的Pyonex针针灸贴在BL-23和BL-34,DU-3和Du-4g以及局部Ahshi穴。

Here the powerful adhesive property of the newest generation of Pyonex is particularly valuable. The length of the needles is selected on an individual basis, but mostly the needle sizes 1.2 mm (blue) or 1.5 mm (pink) are used. The patient’s partner can stimulate the 新的Pyonex with massage and acupressure according to the preference of the patient. Pressing on a press tack that is integrated into the plaster further increases the effect of the needle placement. 最新一代的Pyonex的的强烈粘性非常有价值。按个人需求选择针尖的长度，但则大多数针尖的选择尺寸都在1.2毫米（蓝色）或1.5厘米（粉色）。根据患者的偏爱， 患者的伴侣也可以用新的Pyonex进行针灸按摩。将按压针集合石膏中进行按压能进一步增加针灸位置的效果。

Midwifes have also come to appreciate the benefits of 新的Pyonex during the puerperal period. Application at KI-16 can be effective in cases of problems during recovery or lingering pain after delivery. 在接产时的护士也很感谢新的Pyonex带给他们很多好处，因为针灸作用在KI-16穴位可以有效缓解产后疼痛和产后恢复等问题。

Often a tendency towards pain, tension and restricted range of motion in the shoulder and neck area is observed. Acupoints like GB-21, BL-11 and Du-14 are then considered for acupuncture treatment. The additional use of press tacks has proven beneficial - in particular the application of 新的Pyonex at GB-21 should be considered. 经常可以观察到肩部和颈部的一些疼痛，紧张或者活动受限的趋势时，可以考虑针灸在像GB-21,BL-11和DU-14穴位进行治疗。使用按压针进行一个额外的治疗效果 会非常好，尤其是考虑使用新的Pyoenx在GB-21穴位上。

The midwife is responsible for the care of mother and child during the puerperal period. During the first three months especially, painful infantile colics often present a problem in neonates. After discussion with the mother, using the smallest 新的Pyonex with the length of 0.3 mm (orange) has proven very helpful and effective in many cases in reducing or eliminating colic. 接产护士在负责照顾产后期间妈妈和小孩时，在头三个月期间，尤其是小儿疝气引起的疼痛感常常是新生儿时期出现的问题。对于妈妈们而言，在用于治疗和缓解疝气症状时， 使用0.3毫米（橙色）最小型的新的Pyonex效果非常显著。

Because the use of these miniscule press tacks is considered absolutely safe and hygienic, there are no objections against their use for this condition. It has also been found that the application of the 新的Pyonex at LI-3 for is effective for very restless newborns in helping achieve a general equilibrium. 因为在使用微型按压针是绝对安全和卫生的，没人会有异议。同时也发现应用新的Pyonex贴在LI-3穴位可以让躁动不安的婴儿变得平静是非常有效果的。

In the field of gynecology the application of press tacks has been proven effective for the treatment of conditions like menorrhalgia. Acupoints like SP-6, LI-3, GB-34, GB- 41, PC-6 and LI-4 may be used in a combination therapy of acupuncture and press tacks. In cases of PMS the application of the 新的Pyonex during the last week prior to the expected menstruation at the points LI-3, GB-41 and PC6 is recommended. 在用于治疗像痛经这样妇产科领域的应用按压针同时也非常有效。组合使用针灸和按压针在SP-6,LI-3,GB-34,GB-41,PC-6和LI-4穴位。推荐用新的Pyonex针灸在 LI-3,GB-41和PC6穴位在月经来得最后一周可以有效治疗经前综合症。

The diagnosis of endometriosis is associated with chronic gynecological pain conditions in many patients. Acupuncture treatment gives good effects that can be further improved by the interval application of 新的Pyonex at selected acupoints and Ahshi points. 子宫内膜异位症的诊断是与很多患者慢性妇科疼痛有关的。用新的Pyonex选择针灸在Ahshi穴位可以进一步提升针灸疗法的效果。

In summary, the use of the 新的Pyonex during acupuncture treatment in the field of gynecology and obstetrics by midwifes and gynecologists has been proven to be of great value. 总之，妇产科医生和护士使用新的Pyonex在针灸治疗期间在妇科或妇产科领域都是非常有疗效的。
