Howard, John

L.Ac., Dipl Ac.
Maryland Acupuncture Clinic

I would like to share with you some of my uses of the 新的Pyonex needles and how I incorporate them into my practice. I live and work just outside of Washington, DC, our nation’s capital. Most of my patients are so busy and work long hours that coming in more than once a week is almost impossible. My treatment results have to last a minimum of a week or more. After each treatment I will place a 新的Pyonex needle on the same acupuncture points I used during their treatment. I then send them home and instruct them to keep the 新的Pyonex needles in for one week. By doing this I achieve longer lasting results. This simple technique has had a profound effect on my patients’ health. 我想和你们分享一些我的新的Pyonex针的使用方法和如何将其纳入我的日常练习。我生活并工作在首都华盛顿。我的大多数病人都很忙，并且工作时间很长。 一周可能最多来一次。但是我的治疗结果必须持续至少一个星期或更久的时间。每次治疗后我将新的Pyonex针放在同样的的穴位。 然后我送他们回家并指导他们坚持使用新的Pyonex一周。这样做可以取得更持久的效果。这种简单的技术治疗方式对我的病人的健康状况产生了深远的影响。

These needles are easy to use and cause no pain. The 新的Pyonex needles also have many uses. I use the 新的Pyonex needles not only for pain and stressrelated patients but for other conditions as well. They have opened my practice to a whole new group of patients children. They allowed me to treat children that are either too young to use regular needles on or are afraid of regular needles. I have treated children as young as few years old with 新的Pyonex needles and frequently achieved very good results. Over the years I have found a few clinical pearls when using the 新的Pyonex needles. Some have produced incredible results. I would like to share some of them with you. 这些针很方便使用，并且不会引发疼痛感。新的Pyonex针同样也有多种用途。我用新的Pyonex针不仅针对疼痛和压力大有关的患者，其他状况的患者同样有效。 他们把我的做法向一组全新的病人小孩开放。他们让我去治疗那些年纪太小不能使用普通针头或者害怕普通针头的小孩。我用Pyonex针治疗过几岁不到的儿童， 并通常都能有一个良好的治疗效果。多年来，当使用新的Pyonex针时，我发现一些临床的珍贵经验。有些已经产生了令人难以置信的效果。我想和大家分享其中的一些。

  • Runners Technique© - Within 20 minutes of placing the 新的Pyonex needles on the patient’s ears, it helps them run up to 12% faster than they ever ran 跑步运动技术 - 将新的Pyonex针至于患者耳朵上20分钟，它能有效协助运动员提升原本12%的跑步速度。
  • Scalp Acupuncture - just one or two 新的Pyonex needles are needed to reduce pain levels dramatically for my patients. The results last quite a while and the patient compliance is very good. 头皮针 - 仅需要1-2根新的Pyonex针就可以减少患者疼痛的剧烈程度，持续效果能保持相当久一段时间，并且患者都很能接纳度都很好。
  • Bedwetting - Using SP6 bilaterally on kids decreases bedwetting at night 尿床针 - 在晚上使用SP6双向针能有效改善孩子晚上尿床的毛病。
  • Headaches - Placing a 新的Pyonex needle on Point Zero, Shenmen and Omega 2 in the ear can stop most headaches in just minutes. 头痛针 - 将新的Pyonex针作用于承灵穴，神门穴，欧米茄2耳穴部位。在几分钟之后，能缓解大多数的头痛。
  • Sleep Apnea and Snoring - Using the Chong Vessel (SP4 - PC6) along with points on the ear lobe in the Nogier’s 3 Phases 睡眠呼吸暂停和打鼾 - 使用冲脉（SP4-PC6）沿着耳垂在诺吉的3阶段。
  • Depression and Anxiety - Using the hip point and heart point in Nogier’s Phase 3 on the ear 异域和焦虑 - 使用臀部穴位和心穴在诺吉3阶段
  • Motion Sickness - Placing a 新的Pyonex needle in PC6 for long car rides or airplane flights. 晕车 - 当乘坐长途车或飞机时，将新的Pyonex针置于PC6穴。

I highly recommend using the 新的Pyonex needles. They can be used as a great adjunct to your treatments or as a stand-alone technique by themselves. I use them quite frequently in my practice with phenomenal results. If you are looking to extend your treatment results, or incorporate treating children into your practice, I would definitely urge you to use the 新的Pyonex needles. 我强烈推荐使用新的Pyonex针，它可以作为一个良好的治疗辅助手段或者作为一个独立的医疗技术。在我的实际操作中我经常使用它并且都有一个显著的效果。 如果你正想拓展你的治疗成果，或者将治疗儿童作为你的操作经验，我肯定会建议你使用新的Pyonex针。
