Matsumoto, Kiiko

L.Ac.
NCCAOM certified instructor, lecturer at Harvard Medical School.

1. Our use of 新的Pyonex

We have been using the 新的Pyonex for several years. 新的Pyonex has extended our scope of treatment because it’s become possible to apply press tacks on points on the sole and the tips of fingers and toes, which used to be difficult because these areas are so sensitive. The 新的Pyonex are painless. Also, by applying 新的Pyonex on points used for regular treatment, we have been able to promote and extend the effect of treatment. 新的Pyonex can also be placed on the face without issue. Even if the patient decides to remove them when going out, 新的Pyonex can be reattached when returning home or going to bed and the effect can be prolonged. 我们已经使用新的Pyonex很多年了。由于按压针已适用于脚底，手指或脚趾等一些人体非常敏感的部位，新的Pyonex已经拓展了很多医疗相关领域。 新的Pyonex针可以说是几乎零痛感。同时，通过使用新的Pyonex针已成为按压穴位的常规治疗。并已能够很大地改进治疗的成效。新的PYonex可以 贴在脸上也没有任何问题。即使用户想要外出时决定撕掉它，新的Pyonex也可以在用户回家或上床后重新贴上，并且效果依旧。

2. Clinical Application of 新的Pyonex 新的Pyonex的临床应用

1 ) Application for scars and wounds 伤疤和伤口的作用

In cases with scars, 新的Pyonex can be applied directly on the scar. In the case of surgical scars on the abdomen or chest, two 新的Pyonex can be placed on both ends of the scar. In the case of a new wound, find several tender points about 1cm away from the wound and apply 新的Pyonex.
Use 0.6mm (yellow) 新的Pyonex. 对于伤疤而言，新的Pyonex可以直接作用于伤疤部位。对于腹部或胸部的手术伤口疤痕而言，两根新的Pyonex可以贴在伤疤的两端。对于新的伤口而言， 找到离伤口一厘米的纤弱穴位并贴上新的Pyonex针。
使用0.6厘米（黄色）新的Pyonex。

Comment 评论

新的Pyonex is effective not only for the pain from the wound itself, but also for pulling pain and radiating pain from the formation of scar tissue in local and connected areas. 新的Pyonex不仅对于伤口的疼痛感能起到效果，而且还对抽筋和在局部或连接区域的创伤棉形成的放射性疼痛有效果。

2) Application for eyestrain, strabismus, and astigmatism 眼睛疲劳，斜眼和散光的作用

In cases of eyestrain apply 新的Pyonex on BL-2 and Meizhong (middle of eyebrow) on both sides. In cases of strabismus and astigmatism palpate the areas between and just below ST-2 and the side of the nose. Locate two or three tender points and attach 新的Pyonex.
Use 0.6mm or 0.3mm (yellow or orange) 新的Pyonex. 对于眼睛疲劳方面，新的Pyonex可以作用在BL-2穴和眉中穴（眉毛中间）的两侧。对于斜视，和散光方面，可以作用于在ST-2穴和鼻子两侧。
使用0.6mmm或0.3mm（黄色或橙色）的新的Pyonex。

Comment 评论

Here is a simple explanation of the effects of the above listed points not just for eyestrain from working at a computer screen and increased study time, but also for strabismus and astigmatism. ST-2 is said to be a point for treating “three whites” (the upward displacement of the iris). This means it corrects the alignment of the eyes. This area is also related to the Large Intestine and Stomach meridians. Thus the adverse effects of over-eating and over-consumption of alcohol that fatigues the digestive system and decrease stomach Qi appear here. It also shows effects on the eyes from nasal diseases caused by mouth breathing which damages the ciliary ganglia. Also reflects distortions in the sphenoid bone from poor posture and/or sleeping habits. This can also cause over stretching or slackness in the optical nerve. The above points are considered effective for the variety of symptoms caused by these factors from an embryological standpoint, the mucous membranes of the nose, throat, and the anterior lobe of the pituitary originate from the same place, and thus these points may affect hormonal abnormalities thus their application is even broader. 在此浅析一下，对于上述穴位的效果不仅仅指的是来自于电脑屏幕前工作很久或者说是学习很久的眼睛疲劳，还指的是斜视和散光。ST-2穴据说是用于治疗 三白（虹膜上位移部位）。这意味着它会矫正眼睛在一条水平线上。这个区域也与肠道和胃有关。因此，暴饮暴食和过度依赖酒精的负面影响就是会增加 消化系统的负担并减少胃气的出现。同时也表明了损坏睫状神经节的嘴巴呼吸会造成鼻腔疾病的损伤。同时也反映了不好的睡姿和睡眠习惯会导致蝶骨的畸形。 这也会导致对视觉神经的拉伸和松弛。从胚胎学角度上说，由于鼻腔黏膜，喉咙，和耳垂是起源于同一个地方，因此以上几点可以认为是这些因素而引发的症状。 这些穴位可能会影响到激素异常，所以被广泛应用。

Caution 注意事项

Patients on blood thinners and anticoagulants easily get internal hemorrhage, so thin and short needles are recommended. Also it’s best to obtain informed consent before treating such patients. 由于血液稀释剂和血液抗凝剂的患者更容易出现内出血，因此薄而短的针头是应该被推荐使用的。在治疗之前也最好要获得患者的知情同意。

3) Application for allergies 过敏症状的应用

Place 新的Pyonex above and below and to the left and right of the navel. This is at the 12 o’clock, 6 o’clock, 9 o’clock, and 3 o’clock positions. 将新的Pyonex贴在肚脐的四周，也就是12点钟，六点钟，九点钟和三点钟方向位置。

Comment 评论

The navel is a remnant of the umbilical cord, which is a vital link with the mother before birth. Umbilical blood is so rich in hematopoietic stem cells that it is often used for white blood cell abnormalities. Allergies are immune dysfunctions so one can affect the immune system by stimulating the dormant stem cells in the navel. The upper part of the navel is also connected to the liver, so this could also activate the Chuffer cells in the liver which have an immune stimulating function. Use 0.6mm (yellow) 新的Pyonex. 肚脐是脐带的残留物，脐带是胎儿出生前与母亲的关键纽带。脐血富含造血干细胞，所以常被用于治疗白细胞异常。过敏事实上是免疫功能障碍， 所以可以通过刺激脐部休眠的干细胞来影响免疫系统。肚脐的上部还连接到肝脏，所以这也可以激活具有免疫刺激功能的肝细胞。使用0 .6mm（黄色）新的Pyonex针。

Caution 注意事项

The above points are on the waistline, and friction from belts etc. can cause needles to come loose or move. Sometimes additional tape is needed to secure them, and it would be wise to obtain patient consent. 在腰部的穴位，皮带等物品的摩擦会导致针松动或移动。有时需要额外的胶带来保固定它们，依然需要获得患者的同意才行。

4) Application for chronic tonsillitis (immune enhancement) 慢性扁桃体炎方面的应用（关于增强免疫力）

Place 新的Pyonex on LI-11. Apply on 1 to 3 tender points around the Nagano style LI-11, which is lateral to LI-11 on the margin of the lateral epicondyle (between the Large Intestine and Triple Burner meridians). 将新的Pyonex贴在LI-11穴。作用于1-3疼痛穴位在长野方式LT-11穴周围，这是li-11穴上缘外侧上髁（大肠、三焦经脉之间）。

Comment 评论

The Nagano style LI-11 is located closer to the Triple Warmer meridian. The tonsils protect us from germs that enter our body along with air and food. Weakening of the tonsils can be detected by reactions at TB-16. The Triple Warmer meridian guards the Weiqi and defends Shen. Many chronic diseases are related to function of the immune system, and strengthening the tonsils with LI-11 leads to an increased immunity. 长野类型LI-11位于靠近三焦经络的位置，扁桃体会保护我们免于细菌顺着食物和空气进入我们人体内。扁桃体的虚弱的时候，可以用TB-16穴位检测到。 The Triple Warmer meridian guards the Weiqi and defends Shen。许多慢性疾病都与免疫系统功能有关，并用LI-11穴去增强免疫力来 增强扁桃体。
Use 0.6mm (yellow) 新的Pyonex. 使用0.6厘米（橙色）新的Pyonex

5) Application for otitis media and pharyngealtonsillar hypertrophy 中耳炎和咽扁桃体肥大中的应用

Place 新的Pyonex on SP-7, Upper TB-9 (one third of the distance from TB-10 toward TB-4), and LR-13. 将新的Pyonex贴在SP-7，TB-9上方（从tb-10向tb-4三分之一的距离）和LR-13。

Comment 评论

Just as in the immune dysfunctions discussed above, pharyngeal-tonsillar hypertrophy is caused by germs. Reactions appear at TB-16. Needling LR-13 removes the reaction at TB-16. SP-7 on the Spleen meridian begins with the character “Lo” which refers to water and thus has a strong connection with the kidneys. The reaction points for otitis media (TB-17 & 21) can also be removed by treating Upper TB-9 on the Triple Warmer meridian.
Use 0.6mm (yellow) New Pyonex. 正如上面讨论的免疫功能有关障碍，咽部扁桃体肥大是由细菌引起的。反应出现在TB-16。针灸lr-13可以去除在TB-16的反应。SP-7的脾经始于“LO”字样并指的是水， 因此与肾脏有强烈的关联。中耳炎的反应穴位（翳风与21）也可以通过治疗上TB-9的三焦经进行摘除。
使用0 .6mm（黄色）新的Pyonex。

6) Application for shoulder tension at GB-21 and along Small Intestine meridian 沿小肠经肩部张力在gb-21的应用

Place 新的Pyonex on SP-9 for shoulder tension at GB-21 and, if it is along the Small Intestine meridian, place New Pyonex on eye points mentioned above under (2). 将新的Pyonex针贴在SP-9穴处为了在GB-21穴处肩膀紧缩。如果它沿着小肠经络，将新的Pyonex针贴在上述（2）提到的眼穴。

Comment 评论

Shoulder tension has many causes so it’s difficult to list the effective points. Thus, we will only list the points for the above mentioned two types.
Use 0.6mm (yellow) 新的Pyonex, for the face use 0.6mm or 0.3mm (yellow or orange) 新的Pyonex. 肩部紧张有很多因素，所以很难去列清楚其有效的穴位。因此，我们仅仅列出那些之前两栏提到的穴位。使用0.6厘米（橙色）的新的Pyonex，对于脸上 使用0.6厘米或者0.3厘米（黄色或橙色）

3. Our assessment and Recommendation of 新的Pyonex 我们对新的Pyonex的评估和推荐

We use 新的Pyonex with confidence in our practice because they are hygienic needles causing no pain, and they deliver certain effects. Our basic approach is to use regular needles on treatment points and then to apply 新的Pyonex afterward to support the effect. However, the abovementioned points deliver a significant effect with just 新的Pyonex, so we have introduced their use here. Be advised that applications on the abovementioned points clearly work better when a Nagano and Kiiko Matsumoto style of treatment is performed first. 由于针头的卫生和无痛，并且能发挥出有效的效果，我们对新的Pyonex的使用有很足的信心和很好的操作。我们的基本操作方式是使用常规针头作用于 需要治疗的穴位然后再使用新的Pyonex针进行效果提升的支持。然而，上述穴位都是使用新的Pyonex针后发挥的显出效果。所以我们在这介绍了它们的使用方法。 注意，在上述应用中明确提到了更好的效果时，Nagano和Kiiko Matsumoto的风格是才是先进行的治疗方法。
