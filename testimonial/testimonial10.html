Kuwahara, T. Koei

L.Ac.
President of the Hari Society and the Ki Science Institute
Assistant Professor at the New England School of Acupuncture

Introduction to 新的Pyonex

One of my favorite acupuncture techniques that I have used consistently in over 30 years of practice is the press tack. It’s an important clinical tool for increasing, consolidating, and extending the effect of treatment. The use of press tacks is becoming even more popular today. It is used not only for pain management, which acupuncture is already known for, but more broadly for pediatric treatments, internal diseases, and psychosomatic conditions, as well as health maintenance. This is because a new breed of press tacks called 新的Pyonex has extended its application into many conditions. I would like to introduce some of the advantages of 新的Pyonex and some of the ways in which I apply 新的Pyonex and I hope this will encourage more practitioners to use them. 我最喜欢的一种针灸疗法是我着手于30年一直在使用的按压针。对于改善和扩大治疗效果是很重要的临床治疗手段。如今，这种按压针疗法越来越被大众所接受和受欢迎。 它不仅仅作为一种广为人知的针灸镇痛手段，也更广泛地运用在儿科，内科，精神科以及身心健康领域。这是因为一种被叫做新的Pyonex的新品种按压针已经推广都各个领域。 我会介绍一些新的Pyonex的优势所在和一些使用新的Pyonex的正确方法，并且我希望这能鼓励更多人使用它。

Their Safety and Utility as a Treatment Tool 安全高效的医疗工具

  1. They are extremely easy to place on the point. No special practice is needed, and anyone can attach them securely on the intended acupuncture point. 它可以很容易就可以贴在穴位上，不需要过多的特别练习，每个人都可以安稳地贴在你想贴的穴位上。
  2. Even slight pain is rare when placing these press tacks, so even little children do not realize they are getting acupuncture. 贴上按压针的时候几乎不会感觉到疼痛感，所以在给小朋友做针灸时，小朋友几乎不会有所察觉。
  3. The micropore tape, the needle, and the plastic head of the needle are seamlessly connected so that the press tack is firmly attached to the skin. So the needle doesn’t lift off the skin and cause discomfort even when there is movement. Also it rarely comes off by itself, so the patient doesn’t lose the press tack. 微孔绷带，针头，和塑料针头盖都是一体化的，以便于按压针能够牢牢地紧贴在皮肤上。所以，即便是有任何挪动，只要针贴上就不会从皮肤上脱落也不会造成任何不适。 而且它几乎也不会自己脱落，因此患者不会弄掉按压针。
  4. The miniscule needle is embedded in the adhesive tape and is enclosed in sterile packaging so that it cannot be contaminated in the clinic and the needle is never lost due to mishandling in the clinic. 小针头被放入用无菌的塑封袋里，并用黏胶带塑封，以便于针不会在医疗室弄脏，也不会因为医疗失误而被弄丢。
  5. One big advantage is that there are five different lengths of which 0.3mm is the shortest. The thickness of most patients’ skin is over 1mm, so the needle tip remains securely within the skin. 它最大的优势在于有五种不同规格长度的针头，最短的则是0.3毫米，大多数患者的皮肤厚度超过1毫米，因此针头能够安稳地留在皮肤上。

The ways I apply 新的Pyonex

(A) Cases of pain

  1. Acute cases:
    1. Apply on same point of same channel on opposite side of painful point.
    2. Apply on Jing Well, or Ying Spring point of channel that goes through painful area.
  2. Sub-acute and chronic cases:
    1. Apply on Yuan Source, Xi Cleft, or Luo Connecting point of same channel on opposite side of pain.
    2. Apply on boundary of normal and affected area on the channel either upstream or downstream. Also one can apply on either of these points based on palpating stronger Ki stagnation (tenderness, hardness, coldness, rough skin, etc.).
    3. Apply on a point at the origin and insertion of the muscle in pain.
  3. Chronic pain: In addition to
    1. above,
    2. Apply to Yuan Source, Xi Cleft, or Luo Connecting point etc. on the opposite side on the channel that is the Yin-Yang opposite in the same category (six stages). For example in the case of left baseball elbow (pain on Small Intestine channel), KI-7 or KI-8 on the right can be effective. 适用于元源穴，西裂穴或洛穴相连接的穴位等。在经脉相对，阴阳相对的同一类别（6个阶段）。例如在左侧棒球肘（肠道上的小疼痛），在右边的 KI-7或KI-8穴非常有效。
    3. Apply on most tender point in the affected area.适用于患处压痛点。

(B) Cases of pain from internal diseases来自内科的疼痛

  1. Differentiate the cause and location of the disease based on channel theory. Then apply on the tender Xi Cleft or Luo Connecting point etc. on the healthy side of channel that is in midnight-midday relationship to the affected channel.
  2. Apply on the Confluent Point pairs of related Extraordinary Vessels.
  3. Also apply as explained under 3) for Chronic Pain above.

(C) Other applications

  1. Apply on Back Shu points based on the diagnosis. Apply on these points by palpating tenderness or depression.
  2. Apply on points showing clear difference by palpation method mentioned below. Apply on points that are still tender or tight after treatment.
  3. Apply on points that are known for special effects. For example, GV-12 is effective for colic or hysteric crying in little children (Kanno- mushi).

Do not apply 新的Pyonex on too many points using the above methods. First find and mark reactive points by palpation (press or pinching), give a patient your core system of treatment (like as root treatment in Meridian therapy) and then carefully repalpate these points to reduce the number of points. At that time, change your palpation method. Instead of pressing to reach below the skin, palpate very gently by stroking over the skin. In this way you find points where the skin is slightly thicker, or rough, or where it seems stuck to layers underneath. Apply 新的Pyonex directly over these findings. I call these “Live Volcano Points.” When you learn to use not only tender points, but these “Live Volcano Points,” you can get a 100% effect. And the effect starts immediately after appling 新的Pyonex. The relief and pain will alternate every 2 to 3 hours like the tide, but there will be a clear and sustained effect for one or two days. Another way to maintain the effect is to have the patient gently tap or flick the plastic button on the 新的Pyonex with a fingertip. The effect rapidly diminishes two to three days after placement, so you must relocate the point and apply another one. Effective points move. This is my approach to 新的Pyonex application. 不要用以上方法去使用新的Pyonex针贴在太多穴位上。首先用手指按压要找到并标记上活跃穴位，找到治疗患者的核心治疗方案（就像中医理论的根治疗） 然后小心地再次按压这些穴位来减少穴位的数量。与此同时，改变你的按压方法。轻轻地按压皮肤，而不是按压到皮肤的最深处。用这样的方式你找到 那些很厚很粗或者深深地卡在皮肤下面的穴位上。运用新的Pyonex可以直接的找到这些穴。我称之为“活火山穴位”。当你了解到使用它不仅仅在柔软的穴位，而且还是 在“活火山”穴位。你可以得到100%的治疗效果。而且在使用新的Pyonex后效果会立竿见影。忽痛忽不痛的感觉会在2-3小时期间交替出现。但会有一到两天后就会有明显的持续效果。 另一种保持疗效的办法是让患者用指尖轻轻敲弹新的Pyonex针上的塑料按钮。贴上2到3天后，效果会很快减弱。所以你必须重新贴在另一个穴位上。这就是我应用新的Pyonex的方法。

One last thing. Application of 新的Pyonex on active points along with home moxibustion (using stickon moxa) on indicated Confluent Point pairs of the Extraordinary Vessels is an excellent way to reduce the treatment period by maintaining the effect of treatment. It speeds up the healing process and this increases the return rate of patients, so it’s good for business. Currently 新的Pyonex is a bit more expensive than other press tacks but its benefits far out weight its costs. It is a product that satisfies both the practitioner and patient’s needs, so I highly recommend 新的Pyonex. 最后要说的是，随着国内艾灸在新的Pyonex上的广泛运用已表明了通过持续治疗特殊经脉的聚合穴位是减少治疗周期的好方法。它加快了愈合疗程，并且 这增加了患者的回报率。因此它有利于商业价值。目前新的Pyonex比市面上其他按压针要稍微贵一点点但它的优势远远超过它的成本。它是一种医生和患者的需求上都会满意的产品。 所以我极力推荐新的Pyonex这款产品。
